ELEMENTS CAFE BAR

Open from Wednesday to Sunday from 12h00 to 22h00, elements cafe bar in Umhlanga is situated in Beverley Hills Hotel, and offers eclectic informal dining, light snacks, and a selection of cocktails and beverages.

With its trendy blend of aluminium and marble, statement chandeliers and stylish chairs, elements cafe bar is the place to be seen in Umhlanga. As for drinks, elements is renowned for its unsurpassed range of delectable cocktails. A superb selection of salads, pasta and seafood is available for lunches or early dinners.

Large sliding doors open onto scintillating views of the Indian Ocean. Whether meeting with friends, or simply enjoying a cappuccino or glass of wine, elements cafe is the place to be.

THE SUGAR CLUB

The Sugar Club restaurant in Umhlanga, which overlooks the sparkling Indian Ocean, is a member of the Chaine de Rotisseurs - a testament to the superiority of its cuisine.

Distinguished and elegant, Umhlanga's The Sugar Club is renowned for its consistent culinary excellence and a popular choice for celebrations. The restaurants Wine List is the recipient of the Diners Diamond Award for 2014 and 2015. Every meal is a celebration in the Sugar Club.

The Sugar Club is equally renowned for its decadent desserts that are a fitting end to a truly exceptional dining experience. The professional and intuitive staff ensures that guests enjoy the very best service.
